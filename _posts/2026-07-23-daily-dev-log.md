## Today's Update

Today was essentially a closing day — the kind where work that had been distributed across multiple feature sets converged into archived state. The centerpiece was completing the full eight-unit implementation sequence for thin-vslice-566, the Product Surfaces SPA Mount Loop. Eight units sounds mechanical when listed, but the ordering is load-bearing. The contract freeze comes first because every downstream decision — what the orchestrator owns, what shape the API exposes, what the frontend can assume — derives from it. By the time I was wiring accessible states on the React side, there were no ambiguous assumptions underneath. Whether the accessible states I shipped today will hold up against a real assistive technology audit is genuinely uncertain; the aria patterns are structurally sound but untested against screen reader flows. That's a known gap, not a forgotten one.
